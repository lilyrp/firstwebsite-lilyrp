My Top 10 Favorite Roles I've Played On Stage

For over seven years, I've been an active participant of all theater endeavors. Since COVID was impending early March the spring muscial, Xanadu, was canceled. I've spent the last six months reflecting on my time on stage, a place I severly took for granted. Here is my list of my favorite roles I've ever played, from my most favorite to my least favorite.

  • Mrs. Peacock
    • Favorite Least Favorite
    First Lead Role Had to Learn How to Scream
    Showed My Physical Comedy Chops A LOT of Running Around and Throwing Myself on the Floor
    My Type Cast as a Crazy Old Lady The Fact that I'm Type Casted as a Crazy Old Lady
  • Drake
    • Favorite Least Favorite
    First Time Opening the Show The Audience's First Impression of How the Show Would Be
    Got to Play an Abusive Dad At Least 100 Degrees in the Playhouse
    Quick Changes Ate Me up Every Scene Got to Wear a Hat!
  • Ann Putnam
    • Favorite Least Favorite
    First Time Playing A Real Person The Weight and Pressure of Playing an Historical Figure
    Most Emotionally Complex Role Crying EVERY Night in Front of EVERYONE
    Pretty Much the Main Villian. She Caused the Salem Witch Trials. Very Passive Character, Held Strength through Quiet Moments
  • Mr. Bucket
    • Favorite Least Favorite
